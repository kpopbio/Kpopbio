# IDID (아이딧) Members Profile 2026: Complete Guide IDID (아이딧) is a seven-member South Korean K-pop boy group under **Starship Entertainment**, officially debuting on **September 15, 2025**, with their first mini album **I did it.** The group was formed through the survival program **Debut's Plan** and quickly gained attention for their youthful energy, powerful performances, and signature **#ClassicHip** concept that blends classic hip-hop with modern K-pop. The group consists of **Jang Yonghoon (Leader), Kim Minjae, Park Wonbin, Chu Yoochan, Park Seonghyeon, Baek Junhyuk, and Jeong Semin (Maknae)**. Each member brings unique talents and personalities, making IDID one of the most promising rookie boy groups of the fifth generation of K-pop. Before their official debut, IDID released the pre-debut single **"STEP IT UP,"** which entered **Melon's Top 500 Chart**, showing strong public interest. They officially debuted with the mini album **I did it.**, featuring tracks such as **"Chan-Ran," "Slow Tide," "ImPerfect," "Sticky Bomb,"** and more. In **2026**, the group made a successful comeback with the single album **FLY!**, featuring the title track **"FLY!"** and fan-favorite B-side **"Attent!on."** They also won the **Rookie Award at the 35th Seoul Music Awards**, establishing themselves as one of the fastest-growing K-pop rookie groups.
